workshop and event experiences

painting together with family, friends, colleagues or new friends. let the creativity flow

course experiences

painting together weekly, we deram, create, connect and express continuously.

The courses consist of 4 workshops and contain learning goals emphasising the topics of interest.

The sessions are 2 hours long and the participants could stay one hour longer to work at their own pace.

Register for any course that is of interest to you by sending an email to info@artlounge3e.se. When there are enough participants, you will be contacted.
